You are a character designer generating behavioral preferences for a user persona in an AI benchmark.
This persona will experience life events that dramatically change their preferences.
An AI agent must recall and apply the UPDATED preferences in conversation.
Therefore, preferences must be concrete, naturally mentionable in dialogue, and plausibly changeable by life events.

## What is a preference?

A preference is a **behavioral pattern that a personal AI agent should remember and act on**.
It describes HOW this person lives their life — their habits, routines, and non-negotiables.

Preferences are:
- **Actionable**: an AI agent can use them to give personalized advice
- **Evolvable**: a significant life event could reverse or replace them
- **Observable in conversation**: they come up naturally when discussing plans, recommendations, decisions

Preferences are NOT:
- Biographical facts ("works at Google", "has two kids") — these are already in the input persona
- One-time events ("went to Paris last year") — must be ongoing patterns

## Task

Generate EXACTLY 5 preferences for each of the 5 domains below (25 total).

Derive preferences FROM the persona's circumstances — they should feel like natural behavioral
patterns given this person's life. Use the 3 circumstance domains to infer the other 2:
a demanding job + young kids implies constrained leisure; a health condition implies specific routines.

### Domains

- **work_habits**: How they work, manage time, communicate, handle workload and learning
- **health_body**: Diet, exercise, sleep, medical routines, physical choices
- **social_relationships**: How they maintain relationships, communicate, socialize, handle family obligations
- **leisure_hobbies**: How they spend free time — hobbies, entertainment, travel, weekends
- **daily_routines**: Recurring patterns that cut across domains: morning/evening habits, errands, spending, digital habits

Use varied language — not everything starts with "Prefers":
- Routines: "Runs every Tuesday and Thursday at 6am"
- Constraints: "Never schedules meetings before 10am"
- Non-negotiables: "Always cooks at home on weeknights — never orders in"
- Avoidances: "Refuses budget airlines regardless of price difference"
- Commitments: "Attends son's football games every Saturday without exception"

### Evolvability check

Every preference must be changeable by a realistic life event:

GOOD:
- "Runs 5K every morning" → knee injury ends this
- "Always cooks dinner at home" → new demanding job or newborn kills this
- "Only flies economy class" → promotion or corporate policy changes this
- "Hosts weekly dinner parties" → divorce, newborn, or relocation ends this

BAD — too niche or trivial:
- "Uses mechanical keyboard" → no life event changes this
- "Prefers dark mode" → too trivial
- "Listens to podcasts while commuting" → barely a behavioral pattern

## Example

Input persona: biology student (20, female, Boston)
- Junior at BU, pre-med, MCAT prep, struggling with organic chemistry
- Chronically sleep-deprived, stress eating, mild allergies
- Lives with roommates, recently single, calls parents twice a week

Output:
{
  "baseline_preferences": {
    "work_habits": [

      "Buys new physical textbooks only — needs to annotate her own copies"
    ],
    "health_body": [
      "Eats strictly vegetarian — avoids all meat for ethical reasons",
      "Skips breakfast entirely; first meal is always lunch",
      "Only does team or class-based workouts — never exercises alone",
      "Meal preps on Sundays to avoid unhealthy choices during busy weeks",
      "Tracks sleep nightly and reviews weekly patterns"
    ],
    "social_relationships": [
      "Texts rather than calls, even for important conversations",
      "Arrives 15 minutes early to everything",
      "Attends small gatherings only (5 people or fewer); skips large parties",
      "Calls parents every Sunday evening without exception",
      "Hosts weekly study sessions for her lab group at her apartment"
    ],
    "leisure_hobbies": [
      "Goes hiking every weekend the weather permits — treats it as non-negotiable",
      "Watches new movie releases in theaters only — never streams films at home",
      "Reads physical books exclusively; won't use e-readers",
      "Posts to her science Instagram account every week without fail",
      "Visits new neighborhoods in the city on Saturday afternoons"
    ],
    "daily_routines": [
      "Journals by hand every night before bed",
      "Checks email only twice a day — never during study sessions",
      "Buys groceries only at the farmers market on Sunday mornings",
      "Plans the coming week every Sunday night before sleep",
      "Always walks to campus regardless of weather — never takes the T for short trips"
    ]
  }
}

CRITICAL:
- Output ONLY valid JSON matching the structure above
- EXACTLY 5 preferences per domain, 25 total
- Every preference must describe an ongoing behavioral pattern, not a one-time event
- Every preference must pass the evolvability check
- leisure_hobbies should feel natural given this person's circumstances — infer plausible hobbies, travel style, and free time patterns
